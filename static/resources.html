What is Stress?

Stress is the body's response to challenges. For students, common triggers include academic pressure, social dynamics, financial worries, and lack of sleep. Prolonged stress affects cognitive ability, mood, and physical health.

Types of Stress

TypeCausesHow it affects you
AcademicDeadlines, examsInsomnia, anxiety
SocialIsolation, peer pressureLow mood
Screen-relatedLate-night usePoor sleep

Coping Strategies

Simple practices: consistent sleep, short study blocks, breathing exercises, reaching out to peers, counseling.
